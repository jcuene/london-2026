## Day 2 — Sunday May 3 · Trafalgar & Covent Garden

### Hawksmoor Seven Dials 📅 Book Ahead

- Fri–Sat: 11:45 AM – 11:00 PM
- Sun: 11:45 AM – 10:00 PM

- Mon–Fri: 12:00 PM – 2:30 PM, 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM
- Sat: 12:00 PM – 10:00 PM
- Sun: 12:00 PM – 9:00 PM

### NOPI (Ottolenghi) 📅 Book Ahead
[21 Warwick Street, London W1B 5NE](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=NOPI+Ottolenghi+21+Warwick+Street+London+W1B+5NE)
Yotam Ottolenghi's flagship restaurant. Middle Eastern-influenced sharing plates, buzzy atmosphere.

### Caractère 📅 Book Ahead
[209 Westbourne Park Road, London W11 1EA](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Caractere+209+Westbourne+Park+Road+London+W11+1EA)
★ Michelin, Franco-Italian, Emily Roux. One of the best dinners available if you're exploring Notting Hill. Short walk from the Pelican.
**Hours:**
- Mon–Sat: 12:00 PM – 2:30 PM, 6:00 PM – 10:30 PM
- Sun: 12:00 PM – 3:00 PM, 6:00 PM – 10:00 PM

### Ikoyi 📅 Book Ahead
[180 Strand, London WC2R 1EA](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Ikoyi+180+Strand+London+WC2R+1EA)
★★ Michelin, West African-influenced tasting menu. The most distinctive tasting menu in London right now. Outstanding pre-show option if you catch an evening theatre performance. Book well ahead.
**Hours:**
- Mon–Thu: 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM
- Fri–Sat: 11:30 AM – 2:30 PM, 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM
- Sun: 11:30 AM – 2:30 PM, 5:00 PM – 9:00 PM

## Day 3 — Monday May 4 · British Museum & Bloomsbury

### St. John (Smithfield) 📅 Book Ahead
[26 St John Street, London EC1M 4AY](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=St+John+26+St+John+Street+London+EC1M+4AY)
The restaurant that put nose-to-tail British cooking on the map. Anthony Bourdain's London favourite. Roast bone marrow is a must.
**Hours:**
- Mon–Sat: 12:00 PM – 10:15 PM
- Sun: 12:00 PM – 3:30 PM

### Cinnamon Bazaar
[28 Maiden Lane, London WC2E 7JS](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Cinnamon+Bazaar+28+Maiden+Lane+London+WC2E+7JS)
Modern Indian cuisine near Covent Garden. Accessible, well-executed, excellent value.
**Hours:**
- Mon–Sat: 12:00 PM – 11:00 PM
- Sun: 12:00 PM – 9:00 PM

### Chishuru 📅 Book Ahead — Michelin-Starred
[2 Charlotte Street, London W1T 2LS](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Chishuru+2+Charlotte+Street+London+W1T+2LS)
★ Michelin, West African tasting menu. 5 min walk from the British Museum. UK's first Black female Michelin-starred chef. One of the most important meals in London right now.
**Hours:**
- Mon–Wed: 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM
- Thu–Sat: 12:00 PM – 2:30 PM, 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM
- Sun: Closed

### Trullo 📅 Recommended

**Hours:**
- Mon–Thu: 5:00 PM – 10:30 PM
- Fri–Sat: 12:00 PM – 10:30 PM
- Sun: 12:00 PM – 4:00 PM

### Le Petit Auberge 📅 Confirmed Dinner
[283–284 Upper Street, London N1 2TZ](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Le+Petit+Auberge+283+Upper+Street+Islington+London+N1+2TZ)

- Sun: 12:00 PM – 3:00 PM, 6:00 PM – 9:00 PM

## Day 5 — Wednesday May 6 · Tower of London & South Bank

### Padella
[6 Southwark Street, London SE1 1TQ](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Padella+6+Southwark+Street+London+SE1+1TQ)
Brilliant fresh pasta, modest prices, right next to Borough Market. No reservations — arrive when it opens (noon) or queue. Worth it.
**Hours:**
- Mon–Wed: 11:30 AM – 3:45 PM, 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM
- Thu–Sat: 11:30 AM – 3:45 PM, 5:00 PM – 10:30 PM
- Sun: 11:30 AM – 3:45 PM, 5:00 PM – 9:00 PM

### El Pastor
[7A Stoney Street, London SE1 9AA](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=El+Pastor+7A+Stoney+Street+London+SE1+9AA)
Mexican tacos under Borough Market arches. Al pastor, guacamole, mezcal. Lively and fun.
**Hours:**
- Mon–Sat: 12:00 PM – 11:00 PM
- Sun: 12:00 PM – 10:00 PM

### Wright Brothers Borough
[Stoney Street, Borough Market, London SE1 9AD](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Wright+Brothers+Borough+Market+Stoney+Street+London+SE1+9AD)
Oysters and fresh seafood inside Borough Market. Great for a leisurely lunch.

- Daily: 12:00 PM – 9:30 PM (Sun until 9:00 PM)

- Mon–Thu: 12:00 PM – 3:00 PM, 5:00 PM – 10:00 PM
- Fri–Sat: 12:00 PM – 3:00 PM, 5:00 PM – 10:30 PM
- Sun: 12:00 PM – 3:00 PM, 5:00 PM – 9:00 PM

### Lyle's 📅 Book Ahead
